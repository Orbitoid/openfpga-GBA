Analogizer-maintained fork by Orbitoid.

Based on openfpga-GBA by mincer_ray.
Original core GBA_MiSTer by MiSTer-devel contributors.

Analogizer-FPGA adapter support based on RndMnkIII's Analogizer interface modules.
Use this separate GBA_Analogizer core for analog video output through the cartridge adapter.

Report Analogizer fork issues to https://github.com/Orbitoid/openfpga-GBA/issues.
Do not report Analogizer-specific bugs to mincer_ray or the MiSTer repo.
